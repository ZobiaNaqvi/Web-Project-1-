Intro

Human nature is a complex tapestry woven from threads of cooperation and competition, empathy and self-interest. We yearn for connection and belonging, yet strive for individuality and achievement. This inherent duality fuels both our greatest advancements and most destructive conflicts. It's the ongoing exploration of this intricate nature that makes us endlessly fascinating.
